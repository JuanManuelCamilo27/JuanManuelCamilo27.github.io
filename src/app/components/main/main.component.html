Welcome to My Game Development Portfolio

Over the past few years, I've been diving into various programming courses, but I recently realized I hadn't undertaken any projects on my own. That realization led me to seek inspiration, and I stumbled upon a YouTube video showcasing the development of a minesweeper game.

I thought to myself, what better way to apply my newfound knowledge than by creating simple games? This portfolio is a collection of those projects, born out of a desire to learn and grow as a developer.

Each game here represents a journey of planning the game mechanics and a user-friendly interface, coding, and problem-solving. Let me show you the biggest challenges I faced with each of the games:

  • Minesweeper: Challenges included generating mines randomly and managing the game's state effectively.
  • Memory Game: Challenges included implementing card matching logic and designing clear visual cues.
  • Simon: Challenges included implementing the sequence generation and user input logic. Designing clear visual and auditory feedback was crucial for enhancing the player experience.

Feel free to explore and play the games I've created. Your feedback is always appreciated!
